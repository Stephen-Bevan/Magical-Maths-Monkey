Who Are The Bootcamp Bandits?

Now that's a good question!

We are Stephen, Borys and Emma, three junior programmers based out of Cork, Ireland!

We all remember how much we struggled with multiplication when we were at school. As the Bootcamp Bandits, we want to make apps that make learning easier and more fun for both kids and for teachers, and that's why we dedicated our first maths-themed app towards practicing your times tables!
